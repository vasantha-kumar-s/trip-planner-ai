2-Day Chennai Travel Itinerary

  • Day 1: Visit Marina Beach, explore Fort St. George, and enjoy authentic South Indian breakfast at Saravana Bhavan.
  • Day 2: Explore Kapaleeshwarar Temple, visit Valluvar Kottam, and shop at T Nagar.

Famous Chennai Food

Experience delicious South Indian cuisine such as **Idli, Dosa, Filter Coffee, and Chettinad Chicken** at places like Murugan Idli Shop and Dindigul Thalappakatti.
