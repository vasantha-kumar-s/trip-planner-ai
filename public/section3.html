2-Day Vijayawada Travel Itinerary

  • Day 1: Visit Kanaka Durga Temple, explore Prakasam Barrage, and relax at Bhavani Island.
  • Day 2: Explore Undavalli Caves, enjoy local street food, and visit Gandhi Hill for a panoramic view.

Famous Vijayawada Food

Try the famous Andhra Meals, Rava Dosa, Pesarattu, and Gongura Pachadi at popular places like Babai Hotel and RR Durbar.
